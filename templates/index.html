WHAT IS
CARBON FOOTPRINT

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases emitted by an individual, organization, event or place. The lower your carbon footprint is, the better it is for our environment. Hence by reducing your own carbon footprint, you would essentially be playing a part in saving our Earth.
